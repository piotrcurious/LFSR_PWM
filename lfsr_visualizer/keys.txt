Key mappings (while the program runs)

T — toggle showing transients (negative values)

S — toggle showing stables (positive values)

L — toggle showing lockups (ns==seed)

LEFT / RIGHT — decrease / increase min duty by 1%

DOWN / UP — decrease / increase max duty by 1%

R — reset filters (min=0, max=1, show all)

H — toggle help text on/off

Esc or window close — quit
